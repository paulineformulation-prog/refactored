How It Works

Our streamlined process transforms environmental challenges into agricultural opportunities

1

Invasive Species Collection

Partner with local communities to collect invasive shrubs, creating employment while clearing degraded lands.

2

Biochar Production

Convert collected biomass into high-quality biochar using portable, efficient kilns designed for rural environments.

3

Soil Analysis

Use instant soil testing devices to analyze farmer's land, measuring nutrients, pH, and carbon content.

4

AI Formulation

Our machine learning algorithms create customized fertilizer formulations based on soil data, crop type, and climate conditions.

5

Product Delivery

Deliver biochar and customized fertilizers directly to farmers through mobile units and local distribution networks.

6

Monitoring & Support
